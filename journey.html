Our Journey

September 2024

Just getting started

At the start, we we're just a bunch of students thrown together in a group who has different vibes, different strengths, figuring things out. But as the journey goes, we all started clicking, from awkward silences, to random laughters, to "Ano ulit gagawin natin?" moments. It wasn't perfect, but we were finding our groove.

November 2024

Figuring things out

At this point, Reverie was established and we started to find our groove, kind of. We figured out that all of us have different strengths, some of us were good at organizing stuff, some good at brainstorming, some at coding. It wasn't smooth sailing though, we still had bumps along the way. It was messy at times, but we were definitely getting there.

January 2025

On A Roll

By now, we were really starting to pick up speed. Things felt challenging, but we were getting into a rhythm. Ideas came in easy, everyone knew what they had to do, and we started to make real progress. The teamwork was smoother and there was a real sense that we were making something good together.

April 2025

Full Speed Ahead

As we reached the finish line, it was unbelievable how far we've come. It's just not about the tasks we made or completed, but the journey we just went through. It might have ended, but the memories and lessons we shared would stay with us.
